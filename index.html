About

In addition to being a writer, Nandini Rabindra Maharana (she/her) is an engineering student studying computer science in Delhi, India. She was born in 2004. Her work has previously appeared in Aurora Journal, Ice Lolly Review, etc. She loves reading as well as outlining novels in her idle hours. She appreciates songs from ENHYPEN, hardcore pop, and POV playlists. She loves cold coffee and nature walks. Even though the summer is her favorite season, she writes the least about it.
